## âš ï¸ **Potential Conflicts**

### Mac Users: Command+Q Quits Apps
On Mac, `Command+Q` is the standard "Quit" shortcut. This might cause conflicts.

**Solution:**
1. Go to `chrome://extensions/shortcuts`
2. Change to a different shortcut like:
   - `Command+Shift+Q`
   - `Command+Option+Q`
   - `F9`

### Windows/Linux: Ctrl+Q May Be Used by Other Extensions
If another extension uses `Ctrl+Q`, Chrome will show a warning.

**Solution:**
1. Go to `chrome://extensions/shortcuts`
2. Remove the conflict or choose a different shortcut
